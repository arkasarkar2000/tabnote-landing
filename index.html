Why TabNote helps

Most ideas are small when they arrive. A task. A line. A reminder. A rough sentence. The usual problem is not writing them down. It is the tiny wall before writing them down.

TabNote removes that wall. Instead of opening a separate app, waiting for it to load, and breaking your flow, the note space is already there in the place you visit all day.
